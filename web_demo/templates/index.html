TikTok Content Posting API

Automate Product Videos
for TikTok Shop Sellers

ShopReels helps e-commerce sellers create and publish short-form product videos directly to their TikTok accounts using the TikTok Content Posting API. Sellers connect their own TikTok account via OAuth 2.0 and publish videos on their behalf.

🧪 This is a sandbox demo — running against TikTok Developer Sandbox environment.

user.info.basic

After OAuth login, ShopReels fetches the seller's TikTok username and display name to confirm which account is connected before publishing any video.

video.upload

Uploads the generated product video to the seller's TikTok inbox as a draft. The seller receives a notification, reviews the video in the TikTok app, then posts it.

video.publish

Publishes the product video directly to the seller's TikTok profile with a caption, hashtags, and privacy settings specified by the seller.
