Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Overview

ShopReels ("the App", "we", "us") is a TikTok Shop video automation tool that helps sellers create and publish product videos to their own TikTok accounts. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

  • TikTok OAuth Access Token: When you authenticate with TikTok, we receive an access token. This token is stored locally on your device and is used only to make API calls on your behalf.
  • TikTok Account Info (user.info.basic): We retrieve your TikTok username and display name to confirm which account is connected. This is displayed to you and not stored on any server.
  • Product Information: Product names, descriptions, images, and prices that you provide are used solely to generate video content. This data is not transmitted to any third party.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from TikTok users who view your videos.
  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising cookies.
  • We do not store any data on external servers — all processing happens on your local device.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • The TikTok access token is used exclusively to call TikTok's Content Posting API on your behalf — specifically to upload and publish videos to your own TikTok account.
  • Account info (username) is used only to confirm your connected account within the app interface.
  • No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party.

5. TikTok API Data Usage

ShopReels accesses the following TikTok API scopes on your behalf:

  • user.info.basic — to display your TikTok username after login
  • video.upload — to upload videos as drafts to your TikTok inbox
  • video.publish — to publish videos directly to your TikTok profile

All data obtained through these scopes is used only for the purpose stated above and is not retained beyond the current session.

6. Data Retention

OAuth access tokens are stored locally on your device in a .env file and can be deleted at any time by removing that file. No user data is retained on any remote server.

7. Security

Access tokens are stored only on your local machine. We recommend keeping your .env file secure and not sharing it with anyone.

8. Your Rights

You can revoke ShopReels's access to your TikTok account at any time by visiting TikTok's app management settings and removing ShopReels's permissions. You can also delete your local access token by removing the .env file.

9. Contact

For any privacy questions or concerns, contact us at: uniquestore.us@gmail.com
